About Us

Established on April 12, 2007, Mulamoottil Super Market has been making happy customers for the past 18 years.

Founded by M.K. Thomas (Pappachan Mulamoottil), our supermarket has grown into a trusted name for quality products and excellent service. For the past three years, the supermarket has been successfully led by Shajan K. Thomas, son of M.K. Thomas, continuing the legacy of customer satisfaction and commitment.

With a team of dedicated staff, we ensure a smooth and friendly shopping experience. At Mulamoottil Super Market, we take pride in delivering not just products but a warm, homely experience every time you visit!
